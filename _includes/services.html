Features

Check what can you do with MusicBee Remote

Playback Control

You easily navigate through your active playlist and control playback.

Playlists

You can control your active playlist, add, remove or reorder tracks. Also you can view and add for playback your existing playlists

Library browsing

You can browse the genres, artists, albums and track of your music library.
